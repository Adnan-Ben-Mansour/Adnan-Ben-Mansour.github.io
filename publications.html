    A. Ben Mansour, G. Carenini, A. Duplessis

2022

  1. Federated learning aggregation: New robust algorithms with guarantees

    A. Ben Mansour, G. Carenini, A. Duplessis, D. Naccache

    ICMLA 2022 POSTER

    Proposes robust federated learning aggregation rules that weight clients by the value of their losses, with full convergence proofs validated on IID and non-IID benchmarks.

  2. FedControl: When Control Theory Meets Federated Learning

    A. Ben Mansour, G. Carenini, A. Duplessis, D. Naccache

    ICML 2022 WORKSHOP

    Reframes federated aggregation through control theory. Inspired by PID controllers, we adapt the server step from each client's local performance trajectory rather than averaging coordinates blindly.
